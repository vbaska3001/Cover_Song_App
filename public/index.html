Cover songs are the alternate renditions of an original song. It can be a live performance, instrumental cover, acoustic, acapella, medley etc.. Below you are shown a song and a potential cover of it. Your task is to verify if the candidate cover is indeed a real cover song.

You need to follow a few set of rules to validate it:

  1. It is not a cover song if it's the same audio with other videos in it.
  2. A dance cover, lyrics video, video song, etc. cannot be considered a cover.
  3. A song sung by the same artist in a different event or modulation is a cover song.
  4. Try to be strict with your judgements.
